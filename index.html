About Us

Welcome to the Prodigy Event website! We are excited to host this annual event that brings together students from all over the country to celebrate creativity, innovation, and learning.

The fest will feature a variety of events, workshops, and competitions, all designed to inspire and engage participants. Whether you're interested in technology, arts, music, or anything in between, there's something for everyone at Prodigy.

We hope you'll join us for this unforgettable experience. Check out the event schedule, register for events, and learn more about our college by exploring this website.
